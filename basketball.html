What is Basketball?

Basketball is a sport which consists of two teams that have 5 players each. It should have a basketball court that is 15 meters wide and 28 meters long, have a free throw line, a 3-point line, a midpoint line to divide the court into two, and a center circle.

The height of the basketball ring from the floor should be 3.05 meters or 10 feet. The basketball board should be 1.22 meters wide and 1.8 meters long.

At basketball, you need to learn on how to shoot, dribble, pass the ball, rebound the ball, steal, block, and guard the opponent. You also need to learn to lay up which you will lay the ball up to the ring using only one hand, shoot from the 3-point line, and shoot form the free throw line.
