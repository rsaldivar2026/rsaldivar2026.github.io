NBA

The National Basketball Association, or the NBA, is a professional basketball league in North America that is widely known around the world. The league has 30 teams; 29 of those are based in the USA, while the Toronto Raptors are the lone Candian team in the league after the Vancouver Grizzlies moved to Memphis in the US. The NBA is also popular here in the Philippines, having basketball courts in almost every community that you see. Some players also were wearing NBA jerseys to support their idols.

The League is divided into 2 conferences: the Western Conference and the Eastern Conference, having 15 teams each. The Western Conference teams are Portland Trail Blazers, Golden State Warriors, Sacramento Kings, Los Angeles Clippers, Los Angeles Lakers, Phoenix Suns, Utah Jazz, Denver Nuggets, Oklahoma City Thunder, Dallas Mavericks, Houston Rockets, San Antonio Spurs, Minnesota Timberwolves, Memphis Grizzlies, and the New Orleans Pelicans. The Eastern Conference teams are Atlanta Hawks, Brooklyn Nets, Charlotte Hornets, New York Knicks, Cleveland Cavaliers, Boston Celtics, Indiana Pacers, Chicago Bulls, Milwaukee Bucks, Detroit Pistons, Miami Heat, Orlando Magic, Toronto Raptors, Washington Wizards, and the Philadelphia 76ers.

There are many great players and rivalries in NBA history. The first one is the rivalry between Bill Russell and Wilt Chamberlain, and Bill's team having 8-consecutive championships from 1959 to 1966. The second one is the Magic Johnson and Larry Bird rivalry, which makes Lakers and Celtics a popular and classic team. Magic Johnson and the Lakers won in 1980, 1982, 1985, 1987, and 1988. Larry Bird and the Celtics won in 1981, 1984, and 1986. We also have Michael Jordan in the 90's, which he led the Bulls into the franchise's first 6 championships. We also have another Lakers Dynasty with Kobe Bryant and Shaquille O'Neal in the 2000, 2001, 2002, and Kobe Bryant again in 2009 and 2010. The latest rivalry is between Stephen Curry and Lebron James. During their rivalry, Curry and the Warriors won three out of the four years, which were 2015, 2017, and 2018, while Lebron James and the Cavaliers only had 1 but had an iconic comeback from being 3-1 to 4-3 in the 7-game series of the NBA finals.
